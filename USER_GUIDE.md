# Arnova User Guide

## Welcome to Arnova

Arnova is a premium fashion e-commerce platform offering clothing, shoes, bags, and accessories. This guide will help you navigate and use all features of the platform.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Click "Sign Up" in the top navigation
2. Enter your email address and create a password
3. Confirm your password
4. Click "Create Account"
5. You'll be automatically logged in

### Logging In

1. Click "Log In" in the top navigation
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Log In"
4. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

## Shopping

### Browsing Products

**Categories:**

- **New Arrivals** - Latest products added to the store
- **Clothing** - T-shirts, jeans, sweaters, jackets, and more
- **Shoes** - Sneakers, dress shoes, boots, and athletic wear
- **Bags** - Handbags, backpacks, totes, and crossbody bags
- **Accessories** - Belts, sunglasses, scarves, and jewelry
- **Sale** - Discounted items

**Navigation:**

- Use the main menu to browse categories
- Use the search bar to find specific items
- Filter products by price, size, color, and brand
- Sort by price, popularity, or newest arrivals

### Product Details

Each product page includes:

- **High-quality images** - Multiple angles and zoom functionality
- **Product description** - Detailed information about materials and features
- **Size guide** - Measurements and fitting information
- **Customer reviews** - Ratings and feedback from other buyers
- **Related products** - Similar items you might like

### Adding to Cart

1. Select your preferred size and color
2. Choose quantity
3. Click "Add to Cart"
4. Continue shopping or proceed to checkout

### Wishlist (Saved Items)

Save items for later by:

1. Clicking the heart icon on any product
2. Accessing your saved items from the "Saved" menu
3. Moving items from wishlist to cart when ready

## Shopping Cart

### Managing Your Cart

- **View cart** - Click the cart icon in the navigation
- **Update quantities** - Use +/- buttons or enter specific amounts
- **Remove items** - Click the remove button next to any item
- **Apply coupons** - Enter coupon codes for discounts

### Cart Information

Your cart displays:

- Item details (name, size, color, price)
- Quantity and subtotal for each item
- Shipping costs
- Tax calculations
- Total amount

## Checkout Process

### Step 1: Review Order

- Verify all items, quantities, and prices
- Apply any coupon codes
- Confirm shipping method

### Step 2: Shipping Information

- Enter delivery address
- Select shipping speed (standard, express, overnight)
- Review shipping costs

### Step 3: Payment

- Choose payment method (credit card, PayPal, etc.)
- Enter payment details securely
- Review billing address

### Step 4: Confirmation

- Review complete order summary
- Confirm terms and conditions
- Place your order
